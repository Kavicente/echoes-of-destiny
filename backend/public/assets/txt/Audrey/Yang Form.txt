Yang Form: 
Shadow Shuriken Barrage - In Yang Form, Audrey combines her Claymore Sword with shadow manipulation to perform Shadow Shuriken Barrage. She throws shadow-infused shurikens with deadly accuracy, each imbued with the essence of darkness. The shurikens phase through obstacles and enemies alike, striking from unexpected angles and overwhelming opponents with shadowy tendrils that ensnare and disable.

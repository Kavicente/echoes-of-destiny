Yin-Yang Harmony:
Radiant Shadowstorm - Achieving her ultimate Yin-Yang form, Audrey melds light and shadow in Radiant Shadowstorm. She wields the Claymore Sword and Shadow Shuriken as conduits of radiant and shadow energy, creating a storm of contrasting forces. Light and darkness merge in a cataclysmic whirlwind that sweeps across the battlefield, purifying allies and banishing enemies to a realm of eternal twilight.

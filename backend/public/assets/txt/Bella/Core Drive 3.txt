Core Drive 3:
Healing Crescent - Bella activates Healing Crescent, a skill that combines her combat prowess with her healing abilities in a crescent-shaped arc. She swings her weapon in a sweeping motion, creating a wave of restorative energy that heals allies in its path while dealing damage to enemies struck by the crescent. The healing effect is enhanced based on the number of enemies hit, making it particularly effective in large battles.

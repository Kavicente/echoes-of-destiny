Yin-Yang Harmony:
Lunar Sanctuary - Achieving her ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Bella creates Lunar Sanctuary, a celestial haven of healing and protection. She channels lunar energy through her Mage Gauntlet, creating a radiant sanctuary that purifies the battlefield. Allies are healed and granted temporary invulnerability, while enemies are repelled by waves of celestial light. The Lunar Sanctuary ensures balance and harmony, allowing Bella's team to regroup and strike back with renewed vigor.

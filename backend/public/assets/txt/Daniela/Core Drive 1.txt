Core Drive 1:
Solar Burst Impact – Inspired by her sister Maxine’s solar affinity, Daniela channels condensed solar energy through her gauntlet for a concentrated explosive strike. However, where Maxine focuses on outward power, Daniela internalizes the force to enhance control and precision. Unlike Maxine who risks overheating, Daniela’s precise energy modulation can lead to sudden core temperature drops, causing temporary numbness or slowed reflexes. While this conserves her energy longer, it makes her vulnerable to high-speed or surprise attacks after usage.

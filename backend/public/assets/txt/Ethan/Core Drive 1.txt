Core Drive 1:
Cataclysmic Eruption - When Ethan's emotions spiral out of control, he unknowingly unleashes his most devastating ability, the Cataclysmic Eruption. In this uncontrollable state, a surge of raw energy builds within him, intensifying until it reaches a critical point. The energy then erupts outward in a massive explosion, akin to an atomic bomb, obliterating everything in its vicinity. This cataclysmic blast is fueled by Ethan's volatile emotions and results in widespread destruction, making it a terrifying power that even Ethan fears.

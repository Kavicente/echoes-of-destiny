Core Drive 2:
Cataclysm Blink - After unleashing Cataclysmic Eruption, Ethan's body is briefly swallowed by the chaotic energy released from the explosion. For 3 seconds, his physical form vanishes completely from sight, becoming undetectable to both the naked eye and most energy-sensing abilities.
As the remnants of the eruption fade, Ethan suddenly reappears in a burst of blue-black energy and instantly dashes toward his target at extreme speed. The accumulated momentum from his disappearance is concentrated into a devastating strike with either his fists, stick, or sword.

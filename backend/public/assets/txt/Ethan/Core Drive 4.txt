Core Drive 4:
Soulforge Annihilation - Ethan, equipped with advanced armor, possesses a powerful attack move known as the "Soulforge Annihilation." In this devastating move, Ethan channels a concentrated beam of energy through his armor-clad arms, releasing it towards his enemies. The beam not only penetrates and melts the enemy's armor but also drains a portion of Ethan's life force as a side effect, making it a high-risk, high-reward maneuver.

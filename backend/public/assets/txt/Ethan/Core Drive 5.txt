Core Drive 5:
Flashforge Disruption - Ethan's third attack skill, Flashforge Disruption, is a tactical maneuver designed to disorient and confuse his enemies without risking his own health. In this move, Ethan channels energy through his armor in a way similar to the Soulforge Annihilation, but instead of focusing it into a destructive beam, he releases a sudden, intense flash of light. This blinding light momentarily overwhelms the vision of all enemies within its range, leaving them disoriented and vulnerable to follow-up attacks.

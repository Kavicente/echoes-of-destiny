Yin-Yang Harmony: 
Ragnarok's Wrath - Achieving his ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Ethan channels the fury of Ragnarok into his Viking Sword. He unleashes Ragnarok's Wrath, a cataclysmic attack where his sword transforms into a conduit of elemental chaos. Flames erupt from his strikes, earthquakes shake the ground, and lightning arcs through the air, devastating everything in its path and leaving behind a landscape of destruction.

Core Drive 2:
Shadow Phase - Gwen has developed the ability to meld with the shadows and become intangible, allowing her to pass through solid objects like walls and floors. In this Shadow Phase state, Gwen is nearly undetectable, moving as a living shadow. This ability is invaluable for infiltration, surprise attacks, or escaping dangerous situations. While in Shadow Phase, Gwen cannot be harmed, but she also cannot directly attack, making it a purely strategic skill that requires careful planning and timing.

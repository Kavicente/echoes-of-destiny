Core Drive 5:
Berserker Charge - Harvy taps into his primal rage, entering a berserk state where his attack speed and strength are greatly amplified for a short duration. In this state, Harvy becomes a whirlwind of destruction, delivering rapid and ferocious strikes that can overwhelm even the most formidable opponents. However, this skill leaves him momentarily vulnerable after the berserk state ends, requiring strategic use.

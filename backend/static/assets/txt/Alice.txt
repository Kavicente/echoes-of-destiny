Core Drive:
Thunderous Rampage – Drawing inspiration from her brother Harvy, Alice unleashes a flurry of powerful strikes, channeling stored energy into explosive bursts. While Harvy’s strength overwhelms with brute force, Alice’s version emphasizes precision and radiant shockwaves. Instead of muscle fatigue like Harvy, Alice's body becomes overstimulated by the energy coursing through her. This leads to a temporary euphoric high that makes her recklessly overconfident and more prone to taking unnecessary risks, often charging into danger without strategy.

Core Drive 2:
Luminous Strike - Alice utilizes her single escrima stick to perform Luminous Strike. She channels her inner light, delivering a rapid series of strikes that emit bright flashes. The blinding light disorients enemies, making it easier for Alice to land critical hits and keep her opponents off balance.

Core Drive 3:
Radiant Embrace - Alice performs Radiant Embrace, a healing skill that channels the light energy of her escrima stick into a soothing aura. This aura heals all allies within its range, restoring their health and providing temporary protection against status effects. The radiant energy also boosts their defensive capabilities, making them more resilient in battle.

Core Drive 4:
Sunflare Spin - Alice executes Sunflare Spin, an offensive maneuver where she spins her Handaxe in a wide arc. Each spin generates intense flares of solar energy that explode on impact, dealing area-of-effect damage to enemies around her. The searing light also blinds and disorients foes, making it difficult for them to counterattack.

Core Drive 5:
Eclipsing Burst - Alice unleashes Eclipsing Burst, a devastating skill where she combines light and shadow energies into a concentrated burst. She throws a powerful orb of energy that erupts on impact, creating a field of explosive light and darkness. The burst inflicts massive damage and creates an area of unstable energy that disrupts enemy movements and weakens their defenses.

Yin Art:
Radiant Cyclone - In Yin Form, Alice harnesses her full potential to execute Radiant Cyclone. She spins her single escrima stick at incredible speeds, creating a vortex of radiant energy. This cyclone engulfs enemies, disorienting them with flashes of light and dealing continuous damage with each hit.

Yang Form: 
Solar Axe Fury - In Yang Form, Alice wields her Handaxe with devastating efficiency. She unleashes Solar Axe Fury, a series of powerful, sun-infused strikes that scorch her enemies with each hit. The intense heat of her attacks burns through defenses, leaving opponents vulnerable and weakened.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Eclipse Cleaver - Achieving her ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Alice transforms her Handaxe into a weapon of celestial might. With Eclipse Cleaver, she combines the powers of light and darkness, delivering devastating slashes that eclipse the battlefield. Each strike releases an explosive burst of energy, decimating enemies and restoring balance to the area.

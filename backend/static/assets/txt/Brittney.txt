Core Drive:
Metal Storm - Mastering her combat skills with metal sticks, Brittney unleashes Metal Storm, a rapid and relentless barrage of strikes. She combines speed and precision to overwhelm opponents, striking with unparalleled ferocity and skill.

Core Drive 2:
Crimson Surge - Brittney channels intense energy into her weapons to perform Crimson Surge, a powerful offensive skill that unleashes a wave of crimson energy with each strike. This energy surge causes explosive bursts of damage to enemies within a wide radius. Each successful strike also restores a small amount of health to nearby allies, blending her offensive capabilities with support.

Core Drive 3:
Vortex Barrage - Brittney performs Vortex Barrage, a skill where she creates a vortex of swirling metal and energy. She rapidly spins, using her metal sticks to strike enemies from all directions. The swirling vortex causes continuous damage over time to enemies caught within it, disorienting and weakening them as they struggle to escape.

Core Drive 4:
Shadow Cleaver Assault - In a show of raw power, Brittney unleashes Shadow Cleaver Assault, a skill where she channels dark energy into her metal sticks. She delivers a series of powerful, cleaving strikes that slice through defenses and armor with ease. Each attack generates a shockwave of shadow energy that causes additional damage and creates lingering shadowy aftereffects.

Core Drive 5:
Celestial Healing Wave - Brittney channels her energy to perform Celestial Healing Wave. This skill releases a radiant wave of healing energy that rejuvenates multiple allies within her radius. The celestial energy not only restores health but also removes negative effects and provides a temporary shield, enhancing her team's resilience and support during intense battles.

Yin Art:
Iron Tempest Barrage - In Yin Form agility and proficiency with her metal sticks are amplified in the Zone with the Iron Tempest Barrage. She unleashes a rapid barrage of strikes with impeccable speed and coordination, creating a storm of metal that overwhelms her enemies. Each strike is precise and devastating, capable of disarming opponents and breaking through defenses with relentless ferocity. The Iron Tempest Barrage showcases Brittney's mastery of her weapons, turning her into a whirlwind of destruction on the battlefield.

Yang Form:
Shadow Dagger Dance - In the Yang Form, Brittney enhances her Daggers with shadow energy to perform Shadow Dagger Dance. She moves with unmatched agility, performing a dance of swift strikes and evasive maneuvers. Each dagger strike is imbued with shadowy tendrils that sap enemy strength and leave them vulnerable to follow-up attacks.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Eclipsing Assassin's Fury - Achieving her ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Brittney unleashes Eclipsing Assassin's Fury, a relentless barrage of shadow-infused strikes that eclipse the battlefield in darkness. She becomes a shadow assassin, striking swiftly and silently from the shadows with her Daggers. Enemies caught within the eclipse are drained of vitality and consumed by darkness, leaving them powerless against Brittney's shadowy onslaught.

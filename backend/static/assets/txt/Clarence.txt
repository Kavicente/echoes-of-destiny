Core Drive:
Tech Disruption - Drawing from his expertise in hacking and technology, Clarence unveils Tech Disruption, a skill that allows him to disrupt enemy gadgets and systems. He uses electronic interference to disable opponent's weapons and defenses, gaining the upper hand in battle.

Core Drive 2:
EMP Pulse - Clarence generates a pulse of electromagnetic energy that disables all electronic devices within a certain radius. This pulse can temporarily knock out enemy gadgets, including weapons, shields, and surveillance systems. It creates an opportunity for Clarence and his allies to gain an advantage by neutralizing enemy tech.

Core Drive 3:
Code Breach - Clarence uses his hacking skills to create a temporary breach in enemy defenses. He sends a specialized code that infiltrates and disrupts the security systems of his foes, causing their technological defenses to malfunction. This skill can weaken enemy shields and disrupt automated systems, making them more susceptible to attacks.

Core Drive 4:
Digital Mirage - Harnessing his mastery of technology, Clarence creates holographic decoys of himself to confuse and mislead enemies. These digital mirages mimic his movements and attacks, making it difficult for opponents to target him accurately. This skill provides strategic advantages by distracting foes and creating openings for counterattacks.

Yin Art:
Binary Overload Barrage - In Yin Form, Clarence enhances his hacking abilities to overload enemy systems with binary code barrages. He unleashes a rapid-fire series of hacking attacks that disrupts and disables opponent’s technological defenses. Each barrage targets vulnerabilities with pinpoint accuracy, causing chaos among enemy ranks and leaving them vulnerable to subsequent physical attacks from allies.

Yang Form: 
Nova Sword Overload - In Yang Form, Clarence combines his digital prowess with physical combat using Nova Sword Overload. His Excalibur transforms into Nova Sword, a blade of pure energy that pulses with digital patterns. He strikes with precision, releasing bursts of nova energy that disrupt enemy systems and overload their defenses, leaving them vulnerable to his allies' attacks.

Yin-Yang Harmony: 
Singularity Hackstorm Achieving his ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Clarence executes Singularity Hackstorm, a devastating attack where his sword and laptop merge into a Singularity Blade. He unleashes a storm of hacking torrents and physical strikes that create rifts in reality. Enemies caught within the Singularity Hackstorm are fragmented and disintegrated, their essence absorbed into the digital singularity.

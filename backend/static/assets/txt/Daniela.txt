Core Drive:
Solar Burst Impact – Inspired by her sister Maxine’s solar affinity, Daniela channels condensed solar energy through her gauntlet for a concentrated explosive strike. However, where Maxine focuses on outward power, Daniela internalizes the force to enhance control and precision. Unlike Maxine who risks overheating, Daniela’s precise energy modulation can lead to sudden core temperature drops, causing temporary numbness or slowed reflexes. While this conserves her energy longer, it makes her vulnerable to high-speed or surprise attacks after usage.

Core Drive 2:
Precision Strike - Daniela utilizes her single escrima stick to deliver a series of precise, calculated strikes. Each hit targets weak points in the enemy's defenses, maximizing damage and disrupting their balance.

Core Drive 3:
Restorative Surge - Daniela channels restorative energy through her escrima stick to perform Restorative Surge. She releases a wave of healing energy that restores health to all allies within her radius. This surge also provides a temporary boost to their stamina, allowing them to fight more effectively.

Core Drive 4:
Enhanced Precision Strike - Daniela enhances her Precision Strike with advanced targeting systems. She delivers a series of rapid, focused attacks that not only hit weak points but also generate energy bursts that disrupt enemies' internal systems, causing confusion and additional damage.

Core Drive 5:
Kinetic Overdrive - Daniela enters a state of kinetic overdrive, where she channels all her kinetic energy into a single, devastating attack. She performs a rapid series of strikes with her escrima stick, each hit releasing shockwaves of kinetic force that slam into enemies, causing massive damage and disrupting their formations.

Core Drive 6:
Thunderous Rampage – Drawing inspiration from her brother Harvy, Alice unleashes a flurry of powerful strikes, channeling stored energy into explosive bursts. While Harvy’s strength overwhelms with brute force, Alice’s version emphasizes precision and radiant shockwaves.

Yin Art:
Kinetic Burst - In Yin Form, Daniela channels kinetic energy through her escrima stick, performing Kinetic Burst. She strikes with enhanced speed and force, releasing bursts of kinetic energy that stagger enemies and create openings for follow-up attacks.

Yang Form:
Gauntlet Cannon Barrage - In the Yang Form, Daniela equips her Arm Gauntlet Canon to unleash Gauntlet Cannon Barrage. She fires a rapid series of energy blasts from her gauntlets, overwhelming enemies with a relentless barrage of projectiles that pierce through defenses and cause widespread destruction.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Photon Gauntlet Strike - Achieving her ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Daniela wields her Arm Gauntlet Canon to perform Photon Gauntlet Strike. She channels photon energy into her gauntlets, delivering powerful strikes that release concentrated beams of light. These beams slice through enemies, leaving trails of searing energy and disintegrating anything in their path.

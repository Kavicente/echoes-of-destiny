Core Drive:
Thunderous Rampage - Drawing upon his brute strength and resilience, Harvy executes Thunderous Rampage, a relentless onslaught of powerful blows. He charges headfirst into battle, unleashing a barrage of strikes that can break through even the sturdiest defenses with sheer force.

Core Drive 2:
Earthshaker Stomp - Harvy channels his immense strength into a powerful stomp that reverberates through the ground. The impact creates a localized earthquake, sending shockwaves that knock enemies off their feet and disrupt their formations. This skill is particularly effective for crowd control, as it can stun or disorient multiple opponents in close proximity.

Core Drive 3:
Ironclad Guard - Demonstrating his resilience, Harvy adopts a defensive stance, hardening his body like iron. In this state, he can absorb a significant amount of damage from incoming attacks, reducing their impact. This skill allows Harvy to withstand powerful blows, giving him the opportunity to counterattack with his full strength.

Core Drive 4:
Seismic Uppercut - Using his raw power, Harvy delivers a devastating uppercut that not only sends his opponent flying but also creates a shockwave upon impact with the ground. This skill is designed for single-target devastation, launching enemies into the air and leaving them vulnerable to follow-up attacks or environmental hazards.

Core Drive 5:
Berserker Charge - Harvy taps into his primal rage, entering a berserk state where his attack speed and strength are greatly amplified for a short duration. In this state, Harvy becomes a whirlwind of destruction, delivering rapid and ferocious strikes that can overwhelm even the most formidable opponents. However, this skill leaves him momentarily vulnerable after the berserk state ends, requiring strategic use.

Yin Art:
Thunderclap Counterstrike - In Yin Form, Harvy channels his inner strength and resilience to execute Thunderclap Counterstrike. This powerful special attack involves Harvy absorbing the kinetic energy of an incoming strike from his opponent. With precise timing and immense force, he then redirects this energy back at his enemy with a thunderous clap of his hands. The resulting shockwave not only nullifies the opponent's attack but also sends a concussive blast toward them, knocking them off balance and causing significant damage. This skill showcases Harvy's ability to turn his enemies' power against them, using his own brute strength and perfect timing to turn the tide of battle.

Yang Form: 
Tempest Sword Rend - Drawing upon his enhanced agility and swordsmanship in Yang Form, Harvy executes Tempest Sword Rend. He moves with unparalleled speed, wielding his sword in a whirlwind of slashes that create shockwaves upon impact. Each strike is infused with tempest energy, capable of cutting through defenses and leaving opponents reeling from the force of the tempestuous blows.

Yin-Yang Harmony: 
Thunderstorm Blade Fury - In his ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Harvy channels the power of thunderstorms into his blade. He unleashes Thunderstorm Blade Fury, a devastating assault where lightning crackles along his sword with each swing. The thunderous strikes create shockwaves and bolts of lightning that ravage the battlefield, electrifying enemies and leaving destruction in their wake.

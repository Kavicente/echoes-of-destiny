Core Drive:
Dual Fury Assault - Joy's Dual Fury Assault involves a rapid sequence of attacks with her dual weapons. She combines her speed and precision to deliver a series of slashes and stabs, overwhelming her enemies with the sheer speed and ferocity of her assault.

Core Drive 2:
Serene Edge - Joy utilizes her dual weapons to perform Serene Edge, a precise strike that channels her inner calm into a single, powerful slash. This attack creates a focused wave of energy that slices through enemies, dealing high damage and potentially disarming or incapacitating them. The wave also leaves a tranquil aura that temporarily reduces enemy aggression and increases Joy's evasion.

Core Drive 3:
Shadow Blade Dance - Joy activates Shadow Blade Dance, a skill that allows her to temporarily merge with the shadows. While in this state, she performs a series of rapid, shadowy attacks with her dual weapons. Each strike is enhanced with shadow energy, dealing additional damage and confusing enemies by making her difficult to track. The dance also leaves afterimages that mislead opponents and provide Joy with opportunities for surprise attacks.

Core Drive 4:
Tempest Strike - Joy channels the power of the storm into her attacks with Tempest Strike. She performs a high-speed series of strikes with her dual weapons, each attack accompanied by crackling energy and gusts of wind. The tempestuous force enhances the cutting power of her blades, creating localized storms that knock back enemies and disrupt their formations.

Yin Art:
Cylonic Crane Whirlwind - In Yin Form harnesses her dual-wielding expertise to perform the Cyclonic Crane Whirlwind. She spins rapidly, creating a vortex of razor-sharp crane blades that envelops her enemies. The whirlwind is both graceful and deadly, with Joy's agility and speed making it difficult for opponents to predict her next move and counter her relentless assault.

Yang Form:
Twin Soul Blossom - In Yang Form, Joy enhances her Katanas with the essence of her twin souls to perform Twin Soul Blossom. She moves with unmatched dexterity, striking with the synchronized precision of her twin blades. Each strike resonates with spiritual energy, empowering Joy with the harmony of her dual souls and overwhelming enemies with divine grace.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Eternal Harmony Slicer - Achieving her ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Joy executes Eternal Harmony Slicer, a transcendental attack where her Katanas become instruments of eternal balance. She performs swift and fluid slices that transcend time and space, harmonizing the battlefield with the essence of her twin souls. Enemies are cleansed of discordant energies, leaving behind a realm of eternal harmony in her wake.

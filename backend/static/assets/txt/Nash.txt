Core Drive:
Whip Precision Lash - Mastering the art of whip wielding, Nash employs Whip Precision Assault, a skill that allows him to strike with unparalleled accuracy and finesse. He uses his whip to disarm opponents and control the battlefield, striking with pinpoint precision to incapacitate foes.

Core Drive 2:
Vortex Lash - Nash swings his whip in a circular motion to create a vortex of force. The Vortex Lash generates a powerful suction effect that pulls enemies toward him while inflicting damage from the whip’s energetic arcs. This skill is effective for crowd control, drawing multiple foes into range and disrupting their formations.

Core Drive 3:
Whip Barrage - Nash performs a rapid series of whip strikes, each aimed with precision to hit multiple targets. The Whip Barrage creates a flurry of lashes that deal damage over a wide area, disorienting enemies and creating openings for follow-up attacks. This skill can be used to clear groups of enemies or weaken a defensive line.

Core Drive 4:
Grapple Takedown - Nash uses his whip to entangle and pull in a single enemy, performing a swift and powerful takedown. The Grapple Takedown allows him to close the distance between himself and the target, dragging them into close combat where he can deal devastating blows or set up for a follow-up attack. This skill is useful for isolating key opponents and disrupting enemy plans.


Yin Art:
Tempest Whip Precision - In Yin Form, Nash's mastery over his whip reaches new heights with the Tempest Whip Precision. He lashes out with lightning-fast precision, delivering a series of whip strikes that create a whirlwind of force around him. Each lash is perfectly aimed, allowing Nash to control the battlefield by disarming opponents, entangling them, or deflecting incoming attacks. The precision and speed of this attack make Nash a formidable opponent, able to neutralize threats swiftly and effectively.

Yang Form:
Plasma Whip Surge - Entering Yang Form, Nash enhances his Laser Whip with plasma energy to perform Plasma Whip Surge. He cracks his whip with enhanced speed and precision, releasing bursts of searing plasma that burn through enemy defenses. Each lash leaves behind plasma trails that persist on the battlefield, creating hazardous zones for opponents.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Hypernova Tempest - In his ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Nash unleashes Hypernova Tempest, a devastating attack where his Laser Whip transforms into the Hypernova Whip. He spins the whip with superhuman speed, generating a hypernova of energy that erupts in a cataclysmic explosion. The sheer force of the Hypernova Tempest obliterates enemies and reshapes the battlefield with its overwhelming power.

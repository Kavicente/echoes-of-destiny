Core Drive:
Soulforge Annihilation – Reign unleashes a concentrated beam of pure energy from her Arm Gauntlet, capable of melting enemy armor and piercing through the most durable defenses. However, unlike Ethan’s life force drain, using this ability ages Reign by +2 years per use. The emotional and energy toll visibly accelerates her biological aging.

Core Drive 2:
Cataclysmic Eruption – In moments of overwhelming emotion or urgency, Reign channels catastrophic energy into her body, resulting in a massive energy eruption similar to a solar detonation. The destruction is absolute, but the cost is steep—+2 years of her life are drained each time this is used. If used in succession with Soulforge Annihilation, the accelerated aging could age her into frailty or even death from old age within moments.

Core Drive 3:
Thunder Strike - Reign channels her strength and agility to deliver Thunder Strike with her single escrima stick. Each strike is infused with electrical energy, stunning and damaging enemies on contact.

Core Drive 4:
Electroheal Pulse - Reign performs Electroheal Pulse, where she channels electrical energy into a healing pulse that revitalizes all allies within his vicinity. This pulse not only restores health but also energizes her allies, providing a temporary boost to their speed and reflexes.

Core Drive 5:
Photon Slash Wave - Reign enhances his Photon Sword Slash with Photon Slash Wave. She performs a sweeping slash that sends out a wave of photon energy, cutting through enemies in its path and creating a wave of searing light that deals damage over a wide area.

Core Drive 6:
Tempest Photon Storm- Reign channels the full force of her Photon Sword to execute Tempest Photon Storm. She unleashes a furious storm of photon energy, combining rapid, sweeping strikes with intense bursts of light. The storm overwhelms enemies with blinding energy and relentless heat, leaving devastation in its wake.

Yin Art:
Lightning Barrage - In Yin Form, Reign enhances her escrima stick with the power of lightning. She performs Lightning Barrage, a rapid sequence of electrified strikes that send arcs of electricity through his enemies, paralyzing and damaging them.

Yang Form:
Photon Sword Slash - In the Yang Form, Reign wields her Arm Gauntlet Photon Sword to perform Photon Sword Slash. She unleashes a series of swift, precise slashes that emit photon energy, slicing through enemies with ease and leaving behind trails of searing light.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Photon Storm - Achieving her ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Reign channels the full power of his Arm Gauntlet Photon Sword to execute Photon Storm. She creates a storm of photon energy, launching powerful, sweeping strikes that generate blinding light and searing heat, devastating everything in their path.

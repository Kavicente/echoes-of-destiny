Core Drive:
Spear Piercing Strike - Utilizing his precision and strategic thinking, Ryan employs Spear Piercing Strike, a skill that allows him to pierce through even the toughest defenses with his spear. He aims for weak points in enemy armor, delivering precise and deadly thrusts that can turn the tide of battle.

Core Drive 2:
Crimson Surge - Ryan channels his inner strength into his spear, performing a rapid, thrusting series of attacks in a flurry. Each thrust releases a surge of crimson energy, dealing additional damage and overwhelming his enemies with a barrage of high-speed strikes. This skill is effective for breaking through enemy lines and creating openings for further attacks.

Core Drive 3:
Piercing Gale - By focusing his energy into a single, powerful thrust, Ryan unleashes Piercing Gale. This attack sends a concentrated wave of wind and energy forward, piercing through enemy defenses and pushing them back. The gale effect also creates a temporary zone of turbulence, making it difficult for enemies to maintain their footing and coordination.

Core Drive 4:
Spearfall Rain - Ryan throws a series of specialized spears into the air, which then descend upon his enemies like a deadly rainstorm. Each spear is imbued with energy that causes small explosions upon impact, creating a chaotic battlefield environment. This skill can be used to target multiple enemies and control the area, inflicting damage over a wide radius.

Yin Art:
Sovereign Spear Thrust - in Yin Form channels the enhanced focus and precision of the Zone into his spear combat. He performs the Sovereign Spear Thrust, a devastating thrust that pierces through multiple opponents in a straight line. This attack is characterized by its enhanced speed and piercing power, capable of breaking through even the strongest defenses with precision. It allows Ryan to dictate the battlefield, strategically taking down enemies with swift and calculated strikes.

Yang Form: 
Reaper's Edge - In Yang Form, Ryan channels the essence of death into his Scythe to perform Reaper's Edge. He swings his Scythe with deadly precision, reaping the life force of his enemies with each strike. Dark energy swirls around the blade, draining vitality and empowering Ryan with each successful hit.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Oblivion Harvest - In his ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Ryan unleashes Oblivion Harvest, a cataclysmic attack where his Scythe transforms into the Scythe of Oblivion. He sweeps the blade in wide arcs, unleashing waves of dark energy that consume everything in their path. The souls of defeated enemies are harvested, fueling Ryan's power and leaving a trail of desolation in his wake.

Core Drive:
Shadow Dance - Refining her already versatile fighting style, Audrey unveils Shadow Dance, a skill that allows her to move with unparalleled grace and fluidity. She uses shadow manipulation to confuse and disorient opponents, striking from unexpected angles with lethal precision.

Core Drive 2:
Eclipse Strike - Audrey channels a mix of light and shadow energy into her attacks to perform the Eclipse Strike. This skill allows her to deliver a powerful, focused blow with her Claymore Sword that creates a brief eclipse effect, temporarily darkening the area and obscuring the vision of enemies. The strike deals significant damage and disorients opponents by altering their perception.

Core Drive 3:
Veil of Shadows - Audrey summons a veil of shadows around herself, creating a temporary shroud that hides her presence and blurs her movements. The Veil of Shadows makes her difficult to target, enhancing her evasion and allowing her to reposition or escape from dangerous situations. This skill also provides a brief window where her attacks deal additional shadow damage due to the element of surprise.

Core Drive 4:
Luminous Slash - Infusing her Claymore Sword with radiant energy, Audrey performs the Luminous Slash. This skill unleashes a powerful swing that releases a wave of light, cutting through enemies with searing intensity. The wave of light has a chance to blind or disorient foes, reducing their accuracy and effectiveness in combat.

Yin Art:
Radiant Rebellion Burst - In Yin Form, Audrey draws upon her true sense of self-worth and determination to perform the Radiant Rebellion Burst. She gathers radiant energy around her and then releases it in a burst of blinding light and powerful force. This attack not only damages enemies but also disrupts their focus and coordination, giving Audrey and her allies a tactical advantage.

Yang Form: 
Shadow Shuriken Barrage - In Yang Form, Audrey combines her Claymore Sword with shadow manipulation to perform Shadow Shuriken Barrage. She throws shadow-infused shurikens with deadly accuracy, each imbued with the essence of darkness. The shurikens phase through obstacles and enemies alike, striking from unexpected angles and overwhelming opponents with shadowy tendrils that ensnare and disable.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Radiant Shadowstorm - Achieving her ultimate Yin-Yang form, Audrey melds light and shadow in Radiant Shadowstorm. She wields the Claymore Sword and Shadow Shuriken as conduits of radiant and shadow energy, creating a storm of contrasting forces. Light and darkness merge in a cataclysmic whirlwind that sweeps across the battlefield, purifying allies and banishing enemies to a realm of eternal twilight.

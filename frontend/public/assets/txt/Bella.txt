Core Drive:
Harmony's Grace - Bella combines her combat skills with her healing abilities in Harmony's Grace. She strikes with her weapon while channeling healing energy, simultaneously damaging her opponent and healing herself or her allies. This skill allows her to maintain the balance between offense and defense.

Core Drive 2:
Celestial Strike - Bella performs Celestial Strike, a powerful attack where she channels celestial energy into her weapon. She delivers a single, decisive blow that radiates with heavenly light, causing significant damage to her target. The celestial energy also has a healing effect on nearby allies, restoring a small amount of their health and granting them temporary resistance to incoming damage.

Core Drive 3:
Healing Crescent - Bella activates Healing Crescent, a skill that combines her combat prowess with her healing abilities in a crescent-shaped arc. She swings her weapon in a sweeping motion, creating a wave of restorative energy that heals allies in its path while dealing damage to enemies struck by the crescent. The healing effect is enhanced based on the number of enemies hit, making it particularly effective in large battles.

Core Drive 4:
Guardian’s Embrace - Bella invokes Guardian’s Embrace, a defensive skill where she channels protective energy through her weapon. As she strikes, a protective shield envelops her and her allies, reducing incoming damage and providing a temporary boost to their defenses. The shield also has a passive healing effect, gradually restoring health to those protected by it.

Yin Art:
Lunar Mirrage Dance - In Yin Form channels the calming energy of the moon into her Lunar Mirage Dance. This attack blends her combat skills with her healing abilities, creating an ethereal dance of strikes that both damage enemies and provide a protective aura to her allies. The lunar energy enhances her defensive capabilities while allowing her to maintain an offensive edge.

Yang Form: 
Mage's Healing Wave - Entering the Yang Form, Bella enhances her Mage Gauntlet with healing energy to perform Mage's Healing Wave. She emits waves of healing energy that restore allies' vitality and purge them of negative effects. Bella combines offensive strikes with healing waves, creating a balanced approach that supports her team while keeping enemies at bay.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Lunar Sanctuary - Achieving her ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Bella creates Lunar Sanctuary, a celestial haven of healing and protection. She channels lunar energy through her Mage Gauntlet, creating a radiant sanctuary that purifies the battlefield. Allies are healed and granted temporary invulnerability, while enemies are repelled by waves of celestial light. The Lunar Sanctuary ensures balance and harmony, allowing Bella's team to regroup and strike back with renewed vigor.

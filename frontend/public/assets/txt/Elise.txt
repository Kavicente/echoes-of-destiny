Core Drive:
Blade Dance - Elise uses her single escrima stick to perform Blade Dance, a series of rapid, flowing strikes that disorient and damage enemies with unmatched grace and speed.

Core Drive 2:
Celestial Aegis - Elise performs Celestial Aegis, a defensive skill that channels her escrima stick's energy into a protective barrier. This barrier radiates healing light, restoring health to all allies within its radius while deflecting incoming attacks. The barrier also provides temporary invulnerability against status effects, allowing Elise and her allies to recover and regroup.

Core Drive 3:
Tempest Strike - Elise executes Tempest Strike, a powerful offensive maneuver where she channels elemental energy into her escrima stick. With a swift, focused strike, she unleashes a burst of tempestuous energy that creates a localized storm of lightning and wind. This tempest damages enemies within its range and can disrupt their positioning and attacks.

Core Drive 4:
Vortex Slash - Elise uses Vortex Slash to harness her agility and precision. She performs a series of rapid, swirling slashes with her Xiphos, creating a vortex of cutting energy. The swirling energy slices through enemy defenses, causing continuous damage and rendering foes vulnerable to follow-up attacks.

Yin Art:
Blade Cyclone - In Yin Form, Elise enhances her agility and precision to execute Blade Cyclone. She spins her escrima stick with incredible speed, creating a cyclone of blade-like energy that engulfs enemies, overwhelming them with rapid, graceful strikes.

Yang Form:
Xiphos Frenzy - In Yang Form, Elise wields her Xiphos with deadly efficiency. She performs Xiphos Frenzy, a series of rapid, precise slashes that overwhelm enemies with a flurry of blade strikes, leaving them disoriented and heavily damaged.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Eternal Xiphos - Achieving her ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Elise channels the full power of her Xiphos to execute Eternal Xiphos. She delivers a series of transcendent strikes that pierce through defenses, unleashing waves of energy that devastate the battlefield and leave a lasting impact on her enemies.

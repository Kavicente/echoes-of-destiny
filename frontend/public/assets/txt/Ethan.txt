Core Drive:
Versatile Stick Assault - Ethan's dedication to mastering metal stick combat results in the Versatile Stick Assault. He unleashes a flurry of strikes with his weapon, seamlessly transitioning between different techniques and maneuvers to keep opponents off balance. This attack showcases Ethan's adaptability and skill, overwhelming foes with his versatile fighting style.

Core Drive 2:
Soulforge Annihilation - Ethan, equipped with advanced armor, possesses a powerful attack move known as the "Soulforge Annihilation." In this devastating move, Ethan channels a concentrated beam of energy through his armor-clad arms, releasing it towards his enemies. The beam not only penetrates and melts the enemy's armor but also drains a portion of Ethan's life force as a side effect, making it a high-risk, high-reward maneuver.

Core Drive 3:
Flashforge Disruption - Ethan's third attack skill, Flashforge Disruption, is a tactical maneuver designed to disorient and confuse his enemies without risking his own health. In this move, Ethan channels energy through his armor in a way similar to the Soulforge Annihilation, but instead of focusing it into a destructive beam, he releases a sudden, intense flash of light. This blinding light momentarily overwhelms the vision of all enemies within its range, leaving them disoriented and vulnerable to follow-up attacks.

Core Drive 4:
Cataclysmic Eruption - When Ethan's emotions spiral out of control, he unknowingly unleashes his most devastating ability, the Cataclysmic Eruption. In this uncontrollable state, a surge of raw energy builds within him, intensifying until it reaches a critical point. The energy then erupts outward in a massive explosion, akin to an atomic bomb, obliterating everything in its vicinity. This cataclysmic blast is fueled by Ethan's volatile emotions and results in widespread destruction, making it a terrifying power that even Ethan fears.

Core Drive 5:
Cataclysm Blink - After unleashing Cataclysmic Eruption, Ethan's body is briefly swallowed by the chaotic energy released from the explosion. For 3 seconds, his physical form vanishes completely from sight, becoming undetectable to both the naked eye and most energy-sensing abilities.
As the remnants of the eruption fade, Ethan suddenly reappears in a burst of blue-black energy and instantly dashes toward his target at extreme speed. The accumulated momentum from his disappearance is concentrated into a devastating strike with either his fists, stick, or sword.

Core Drive 6:
Cataclysm Overdrive -  Ethan releases and immediately reabsorbs a controlled portion of Cataclysmic Eruption's energy, empowering his body, armor, and weapons beyond their normal limits. While active, his strength, speed, and attack power are greatly enhanced, causing his fists and weapons to unleash explosive shockwaves and devastating destructive force with every strike.

Core Drive 7:
Empyreal Fury - Ethan's "Soulforge Annihilation" undergoes a transformation when fueled by heightened emotions, specifically the powerful emotion of defeat. As Ethan faces challenges and adversity, the beam emanating from his hand transforms into the "Emotion-Fueled Nova." This enhanced attack move not only counters Eclipse of Hope's destructive Cataclysmic Eruption but also gains the ability to move through and penetrate Ethan Eclipse of Hope's body.

Core Drive 8:
Prismatic Convergence  - Ethan’s Prismatic Convergence is a powerful ability that channels the long-range skills of his allies into a single, overwhelming attack. When his allies—Harvy, Clarence, James, Ryan, Nash, Audrey, Maxine, Joy, Elise, Gwen, Brittney, Bella, Alice, Daniela, and Reign—fire their long-range abilities at Ethan, his left hand armor absorbs these energies, combining them with his own Annihilation Beam.

Yin Art:
Celestial Harmony Cascade - In Yin Form, Ethan synchronizes his movements with the flow of celestial energy, creating a mesmerizing display of coordinated strikes with his enhanced crane weapon. As he moves, ethereal lights cascade around him, amplifying the power of his attacks. Each strike carries a harmonious blend of physical force and mystical energy, overwhelming his enemies with both brute strength and celestial might.

Yang Form: 
Nordic Fury - In Yang Form, Ethan wields his Viking Sword with unparalleled skill and strength. He performs Nordic Fury, a series of powerful slashes and thrusts imbued with the spirit of ancient Norse warriors. Each strike is accompanied by icy winds and swirling snow, freezing enemies in their tracks and dealing frostbite-like damage over time.

Yin-Yang Harmony: 
Ragnarok's Wrath - Achieving his ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Ethan channels the fury of Ragnarok into his Viking Sword. He unleashes Ragnarok's Wrath, a cataclysmic attack where his sword transforms into a conduit of elemental chaos. Flames erupt from his strikes, earthquakes shake the ground, and lightning arcs through the air, devastating everything in its path and leaving behind a landscape of destruction.

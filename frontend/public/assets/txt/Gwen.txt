Core Drive:
Swift Strike - Switching her focus to hand-to-hand combat, Gwen develops Swift Strike, a lightning-fast series of punches and kicks. She combines her agility with precise strikes, overwhelming opponents with a flurry of blows before they can react.

Core Drive 2:
Shadow Phase - Gwen has developed the ability to meld with the shadows and become intangible, allowing her to pass through solid objects like walls and floors. In this Shadow Phase state, Gwen is nearly undetectable, moving as a living shadow. This ability is invaluable for infiltration, surprise attacks, or escaping dangerous situations. While in Shadow Phase, Gwen cannot be harmed, but she also cannot directly attack, making it a purely strategic skill that requires careful planning and timing.

Core Drive 3:
Phantom Strike - Combining her agility with her mastery of shadows, Gwen executes Phantom Strike, a stealthy attack that allows her to strike from unexpected angles. In this move, she uses the shadows to momentarily vanish from sight, only to reappear behind her opponent with a powerful, focused strike aimed at their most vulnerable spots. This skill is perfect for ambushes or quickly taking down key targets without giving them a chance to react.

Core Drive 4:
Spectral Dancer - Gwen's speed and agility reach new heights with Spectral Dancer. In this evasive technique, she performs a series of rapid, unpredictable movements, leaving behind afterimages that confuse her enemies. These afterimages make it difficult for foes to land a hit on her, while Gwen can seamlessly transition from dodging to launching counterattacks. Spectral Dancer turns the tide of battle by making Gwen almost impossible to pin down while punishing any attempt to strike her.

Yin Art:
Cyclone Grapple Barrage - In Yin Form, Gwen combines her agility and grappling skills to execute the Cyclone Grapple Barrage. She moves with incredible speed, creating a whirlwind of strikes and grapples that leave her opponents disoriented and vulnerable. This flurry of attacks is both precise and relentless, allowing Gwen to control the battlefield with her swift and calculated movements.

Yang Form:
Gauntlet Blitz - In the Yang Form, Gwen enhances her Gauntlet with elemental energies to perform Gauntlet Blitz. She unleashes a flurry of punches and kicks infused with elemental power, striking with precision and overwhelming speed. Each strike releases elemental bursts that disrupt enemy formations and create elemental hazards on the battlefield.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Cyclone Gauntlet Storm - In her ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Gwen channels the forces of nature into her Gauntlet to execute Cyclone Gauntlet Storm. She spins and strikes with the force of a cyclone, generating whirlwinds of elemental energy that engulf enemies. The elemental storm creates chaos on the battlefield, bending the elements to Gwen's will and leaving foes battered by the relentless fury of nature.

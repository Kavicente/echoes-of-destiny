Core Drive:
Aerial Assault - Capitalizing on his acrobatic abilities, James executes Aerial Assault, a daring maneuver that sees him leaping into the air and raining down a barrage of strikes on his foes. He uses his agility to evade enemy attacks while delivering devastating blows from above.

Core Drive 2:
Celestial Strike - James channels the power of the cosmos into a single, powerful strike with his weapon. Celestial Strike delivers a concentrated beam of solar energy that pierces through enemy defenses, causing an explosion of radiant energy on impact. This skill is highly effective against heavily armored foes and can cause a temporary sun-blind effect.

Core Drive 3:
Solar Mirage - By harnessing solar energy, James creates illusionary duplicates of himself that shimmer with the light of the sun. These mirages mimic his movements and attacks, confusing enemies and making it difficult for them to pinpoint his real location. This skill provides both offensive and defensive advantages by creating openings for attacks and evading counterattacks.

Core Drive 4:
Radiant Flare Kick - James performs a high-flying kick imbued with solar energy, releasing a burst of radiant flare upon impact. The flare causes a fiery explosion that can knock back and disorient enemies. The heat from the flare can cause burn damage over time, making this skill effective for crowd control and breaking through enemy lines.


Yin Art:
Solor Crance Eruption - While in Yin Form James channels the power of the sun into his crane weapon. With a focused burst of energy, he performs the Solar Crane Eruption, where his weapon glows with intense heat and light. He then unleashes a powerful series of attacks that emit solar flares upon impact, scorching his enemies and creating blinding flashes that disorient them.

Yang Form: 
Solar Scimitar Dance - Entering the Yang Form, James infuses his Scimitar with solar energy to perform Solar Scimitar Dance. He moves with grace and fluidity, executing a dazzling dance of slashes and spins that harness the power of the sun. Each strike releases bursts of solar flares, blinding enemies and scorching them with intense heat.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Eclipse Blade Tempest - In his ultimate Yin-Yang Form, James unleashes Eclipse Blade Tempest, a devastating onslaught where his Scimitar eclipses the sun's light. He combines the powers of light and darkness, unleashing an unstoppable storm of slashes that eclipse the battlefield in shadow. Enemies caught within the tempest are drained of energy and hope, facing the full force of celestial balance.

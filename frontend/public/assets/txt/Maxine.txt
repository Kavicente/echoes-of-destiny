Core Drive:
Solar Burst Impact - Harnessing her strength and determination, Maxine unleashes Solar Burst Impact, a devastating attack that sends shockwaves through the battlefield. She focuses her energy into a powerful strike, creating an explosion of solar energy that damages enemies in a wide radius.

Core Drive 2:
Radiant Strike - Maxine channels solar energy into her Tachi to perform the Radiant Strike. This attack involves a powerful, focused swing that releases a concentrated beam of light upon impact. The beam sears through enemies, dealing high damage and potentially causing burns that persist over time. The attack also illuminates the area, revealing hidden enemies or traps.

Core Drive 3:
Solar Shield - Maxine summons a protective barrier of solar energy around herself with Solar Shield. This shield absorbs incoming attacks and converts a portion of the damage into solar energy, which she can use to empower her subsequent attacks. The shield provides temporary invulnerability and radiates heat, damaging nearby enemies who come into close contact with it.

Core Drive 4:
Blazing Fury - Maxine channels intense solar energy into a rapid series of strikes known as Blazing Fury. She performs a flurry of fast, powerful slashes with her Tachi, each strike emitting bursts of solar flares. These flares explode upon impact, creating an area of intense heat that continues to burn enemies and make the battlefield hazardous for them.

Yin Art:
Solar Crane Surge - In Yin Form, harnesses solar energy to power her crane weapon. With the Solar Crane Surge, she channels this energy into a devastating surge of strikes. Her weapon glows with intense solar energy, and each hit delivers explosive force and radiant heat, overwhelming her opponents and breaking through their defenses.

Yang Form: 
Divine Tachi Barrage - In Yang Form, Maxine channels divine energy into her Tachi to perform Divine Tachi Barrage. She strikes with unparalleled speed and precision, delivering a barrage of strikes that radiate with divine light. Each slash purifies enemies of darkness, restoring balance and weakening their defenses against subsequent attacks.

Yin-Yang Harmony:
Celestial Nova Blade - In her ultimate Yin-Yang Form, Maxine unleashes Celestial Nova Blade, a transcendent attack where her Tachi becomes a conduit of celestial energy. She swings the blade in arcs of radiant light, unleashing celestial novas that engulf enemies in cleansing fire. The celestial energy purifies the battlefield, restoring harmony and vanquishing darkness with each strike.
